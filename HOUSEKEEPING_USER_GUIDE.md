# Tanah Mympi Housekeeping — Staff User Guide

A simple, step-by-step guide to using the housekeeping app on your phone or tablet.
No app to install — you just open a web link.

> **Last updated:** 2026-06-29. The shareable copy is `HOUSEKEEPING_USER_GUIDE.pdf`.

---

## What the app is for
You use the app to **sign off rooms after you clean them**, and to **flag anything that needs
attention** (maintenance, damage, missing items). Your manager can see everything you submit.

**App link:** https://pjcoxy.github.io/tanah-mympi-staff/
*(Tip: open it once, then add it to your home screen so it's one tap next time.)*

---

## Before you start
- You need your **name** and **4-digit PIN** (ask your manager if you don't have one).
- You need an **internet connection** to log in and submit.

---

## 1. Log in

![Login screen](docs/screenshots/login.png)

1. Open the app link.
2. **Choose your name** from the list.
3. **Enter your PIN.**
4. Tap **Sign in**.

If it says *"Incorrect PIN"*, check you picked the right name and try again. Still stuck? See your manager.

---

## 2. ⚠️ Check it says YOUR name

![Sign-off form](docs/screenshots/form.png)

After you log in, a **gold bar at the top shows who is logged in** — for example
**"Logged in as PETE C"**.

**Always check this is your name before you start**, especially on a shared phone or tablet. If it's
someone else's name, tap **"Not you? Sign out"** and log in as yourself. This makes sure rooms are
recorded under the right person.

---

## 3. Sign off a room
Work through the steps on the screen — they appear one at a time:

1. **Select the room** you cleaned.
2. **Choose the service type** (Full Clean, Refresh, Turndown, or Inspection). The checklist changes
   to match — you'll only see the items that apply.
3. **Tick each item** as you confirm it's done.
4. **Flag any issues** if needed (see next section).
5. Tap **Submit**.

You'll see a confirmation when it's saved.

---

## 4. Flagging an issue
If something needs follow-up, tick the matching flag:

- 🔧 **Maintenance** — something needs fixing
- ⚠️ **Damage** — something is broken or damaged
- 📋 **Missing item** — something should be there but isn't
- ❓ **Other** — anything else worth noting

**If you flag an issue you must add a short note** describing it (e.g. "shower tap dripping"), then submit.

---

## 5. Made a mistake? Recall it
Right after you submit, there's a short window where you can **Recall** the last sign-off to fix it.
Use this if you picked the wrong room or service type. After the window closes, ask your manager to
correct it.

---

## 6. If you're offline
If your device loses internet, you'll see a warning and **you won't be able to submit**. Move to where
you have a signal and try again. Don't worry — you won't lose your ticks while you wait.

---

## 7. Signing out
- Tap **Sign out** when you're done, especially on a shared device.
- For your security, the app **logs you out automatically after 20 minutes** of no activity.

---

## Common questions

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| I forgot my PIN | Ask your manager — they can look it up or reset it. |
| It won't let me submit | Check your internet connection; you must be online to submit. |
| The top bar shows the wrong name | Tap "Not you? Sign out" and log in as yourself. |
| I submitted the wrong room | Use **Recall** straight away, or tell your manager. |
| Do I need to install anything? | No — it's just a web link. Add it to your home screen for quick access. |

---

## For managers — viewing the stats

![Dashboard](docs/screenshots/dashboard.png)

Open the **dashboard** to see charts of activity:
**https://pjcoxy.github.io/tanah-mympi-staff/dashboard.html**

It shows rooms cleaned per week (with a trend line), totals per staff member, the mix of service
types, issues flagged, the busiest days, and the busiest rooms. It updates automatically from what
staff submit — just refresh the page.
